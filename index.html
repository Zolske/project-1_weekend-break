faq

Can I bring my pet?

You can bring your pet on the "relax pack" and "discovery pack" tour. But we do not recommend to bring any animals to the "action pack" tour.

Can I cancel my trip?

We understand that plans can change. You get a full refound, if you can give us 1 week notice.

How can I stay up to date?
